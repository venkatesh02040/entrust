About the service

Selecting the right furniture, materials, and finishes is one of the most impactful decisions in any interior design project. These elements define the texture, warmth, durability, and personality of a space. Our expert curators guide you through a carefully considered selection process, helping you choose pieces and finishes that are not only beautiful but also practical and long-lasting.

With access to an extensive network of premium suppliers, local craftspeople, and international brands, we source furniture and materials that match your design vision and budget. From custom-crafted joinery to handpicked soft furnishings, every selection is made with purpose and precision.

Furniture Curation

Handpicked furniture selections tailored to your style, space, and budget requirements.

Material Sourcing

Access to premium local and international material suppliers for every project need.

Finish & Texture Pairing

Expert guidance on combining finishes and textures for cohesive, stunning interiors.

Custom Fabrication

Bespoke furniture and joinery designed and crafted to your exact specifications.

Materials We Work With

We work with an extensive range of materials including natural stone, engineered wood, solid timber, metal alloys, glass, ceramics, laminates, veneers, wallcoverings, upholstery fabrics, and sustainable eco-materials. Our team carefully evaluates the performance characteristics of each material durability, maintenance requirements, aesthetic appeal before recommending it for your project.

Key Considerations in Selection

The selection process involves balancing several important factors: the functional demands of the space, the desired aesthetic, budget constraints, maintenance practicality, environmental sustainability, and long-term durability. Our designers provide samples, mood boards, and detailed specifications to help you make confident, informed choices that you will be happy with for years to come.

  • Access to an extensive network of premium suppliers.
  • On-trend selections that also stand the test of time.
  • Expertise in sustainable and eco-friendly materials.
  • Custom furniture design and fabrication available.
  • Flexible selection options for all budget ranges.
  • Personalised support throughout the selection process.
  • Experienced procurement team managing all orders.
  • We ensure every piece meets our quality standards.

The materials and furnishings you choose are the physical embodiment of your design vision. At Entrust, we take the selection process seriously, combining deep product knowledge, supplier relationships, and design expertise to ensure every element of your interior is perfectly chosen.
