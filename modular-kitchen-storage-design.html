About the service

Modular kitchen and storage design transforms the most functional room in your home into a space that is as beautiful as it is efficient. Our specialists design kitchens that are precisely tailored to your cooking habits, spatial constraints, and aesthetic vision combining high-quality modular cabinetry systems with thoughtful storage solutions that keep every utensil, appliance, and ingredient perfectly organised and within easy reach.

A well-designed modular kitchen is more than just a collection of cabinets and countertops. It is a carefully engineered workflow environment where ergonomics, material quality, and visual cohesion all play equally important roles. We work with a curated range of premium modular systems and bespoke joinery options, giving you access to both the flexibility of modular design and the precision of custom fabrication.

Custom Layout Planning

Ergonomically optimised kitchen layouts L-shaped, U-shaped, galley, or island designed around your unique cooking workflow.

Modular Cabinet Systems

Premium modular cabinetry in a wide range of finishes, from matte lacquer and wood veneer to high-gloss and textured laminates.

Smart Storage Solutions

Innovative pull-out drawers, corner carousels, tall pantry units, and integrated appliance housings that maximise every centimetre of available space.

Countertop & Hardware

Expert selection of countertop materials quartz, granite, solid surface, and compact laminate paired with premium hardware and sink specifications.

Kitchen Styles We Design

We design modular kitchens across every style spectrum from sleek contemporary minimalist kitchens with handleless cabinets and integrated appliances, to warm transitional kitchens featuring shaker-style doors, stone countertops, and open shelving. We also design traditional kitchens with detailed mouldings and classic hardware, and industrial-style kitchens combining metal, exposed brick textures, and raw timber. Whatever your preference, our team develops a design that feels authentic to your home and lifestyle.

Key Elements of Kitchen Design

A successful modular kitchen design balances several interdependent elements: the kitchen work triangle (the relationship between hob, sink, and refrigerator), storage hierarchy (placing the most-used items at the most accessible positions), material durability, ease of cleaning, lighting (task, ambient, and accent), ventilation, and electrical point planning. Our designers integrate all these considerations into a cohesive plan that functions flawlessly from the very first day of use.

Frequently asked questions

A modular kitchen is built from standardised pre-manufactured cabinet modules that are combined and configured to suit your space. A custom kitchen is built entirely to bespoke dimensions from scratch. Modular kitchens offer faster lead times and more predictable pricing, while custom kitchens allow for complete flexibility in dimensions and design. We offer both approaches and can also combine the two for the best of both worlds.

The timeline depends on the scope of the project. Design and manufacturing typically takes four to six weeks after design sign-off. Installation of a standard kitchen generally takes three to five working days. We provide a clear project schedule at the outset and coordinate all related trades plumbing, electrical, tiling to ensure a smooth, sequenced installation.

The right countertop depends on your budget, cooking habits, and aesthetic preference. Quartz is our most popular recommendation for its durability, low maintenance, and wide range of colours. Granite is excellent for those who want a natural stone look with good heat resistance. Compact laminate is an affordable and highly practical option. For premium projects, we also specify Dekton, sintered stone, and solid surfaces. We provide material samples and detailed guidance to help you choose with confidence.

The same principles of ergonomic organisation and high-quality construction that we apply to kitchens are brought to every storage project we undertake.
