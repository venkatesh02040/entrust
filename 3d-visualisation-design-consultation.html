About the service

3D visualisation and design consultation bridges the gap between imagination and reality. Our advanced rendering technology allows us to create photorealistic visualisations of your interior before a single nail is driven. You can see exactly how your space will look every colour, texture, material, and piece of furniture rendered in stunning detail giving you complete confidence before committing to any design decisions.

Our design consultation service provides expert guidance at every stage of your project. Whether you need a one-time strategic session to clarify your vision or ongoing design support throughout an extended project, our experienced designers are here to listen, advise, and inspire.

Photorealistic Renders

Stunning high-resolution 3D renders that bring your interior vision to life before execution.

Virtual Walkthroughs

Immersive 360 degree virtual tours allowing you to experience your space before it is built.

Design Consultation

Expert one-on-one design sessions providing clear direction for your interior project.

Revision & Refinement

Iterative design refinement based on your feedback until the vision is exactly right.

What 3D Visualisation Includes

Our 3D visualisation service includes detailed modelling of your space, application of selected materials and finishes, furniture placement, lighting simulation, and photorealistic rendering from multiple viewpoints. We also offer animated walkthroughs and virtual reality experiences for larger projects. These tools are invaluable for helping clients, contractors, and project stakeholders align on the design vision before work begins.

Key Benefits of Design Consultation

Design consultation helps you avoid costly mistakes by providing expert perspective early in the process. Our consultants help you define your style, establish a realistic budget, prioritise design decisions, and develop a clear project roadmap. Whether you are embarking on a new build, renovation, or simply refreshing a room, a design consultation ensures you start with a solid, well-considered plan.

  • Virtual reality walkthroughs available for larger projects.
