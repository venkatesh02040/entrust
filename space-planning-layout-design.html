About the service

Space planning and layout design is the foundation of every successful interior project. It involves the thoughtful arrangement of furniture, pathways, zones, and functional areas within a given space to maximise efficiency, comfort, and flow. Good space planning ensures that every square foot serves a purpose.

Whether designing a new space from scratch or reconfiguring an existing one, our space planning experts analyse your needs, daily routines, and spatial constraints to create an optimised layout. We use precision tools and software to develop floor plans that make the best use of available space.

Zone Planning

Defining distinct functional zones within a space for maximum usability.

Traffic Flow Analysis

Optimising movement pathways to ensure comfort and accessibility throughout.

Furniture Arrangement

Strategic placement of furniture to balance aesthetics and practical function.

Scale & Proportion

Ensuring all design elements are perfectly scaled to the dimensions of the space.

Types of Spaces We Plan

We provide space planning services for residential homes, apartments, commercial offices, retail stores, restaurants, healthcare facilities, and hospitality venues. Every space type comes with unique challenges, and our team is equipped with the expertise to address them. We create layouts that support the specific activities and workflows of each space type.

Key Elements of Space Planning

Effective space planning involves careful consideration of circulation patterns, natural and artificial lighting, structural elements, storage requirements, acoustic zones, and future scalability. Our team integrates all these factors to deliver a layout that not only looks great on paper but functions flawlessly in real life.
